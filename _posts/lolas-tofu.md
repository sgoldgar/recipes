## Ingredients: 
- 1 block of tofu
- Olive oil
- Soy sauce
- Hoisin
- Turmeric
- Lime
- Maple Syrup


## Insructions:

1. Press the tofu
2. Cut it up and let it marinade in a bag
3. Throw it on a baking sheet and bake on 400 until crispy
