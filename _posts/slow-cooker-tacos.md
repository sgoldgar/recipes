## Ingredients: 
- 1 can black beans not drained
- 1 can corn drained
- 4 chicken breasts
- 1 can whole tomatoes
- 1 can chipotle in adobo
- Half a packet of Mexican spice blend


## Insructions:

1. Dump all in to slow cooker and stir
2. Cook on high for 4 hours
3. Shred and eat
